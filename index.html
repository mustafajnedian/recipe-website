Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Pancetta
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti.
  2. Mix eggs and cheese.
  3. Fry the pancetta.
  4. Combine everything and serve.

Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist chocolate cake perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix.
  4. Bake in the oven.
